New Piece

1
Tap New Piece on the home screen (leave the toggle on Add new)
2
1.1 Basics — optionally start from a Drive folder (search & pick → names the piece and links its photos), then reference name, year, type, price
3
1.2 Shared details — season, occasion, colors, pattern, materials, plant name, and the product description (tap Fix to tidy wording)
4
1.3 / 1.4 Category fields — fill the "these publish to Shopify" set for this type; the other type's set is optional (still handy for Meta/Etsy)
5
1.5 Assign EB number — assigned automatically at the end; print the QR tag and attach it
6
Enter dimensions + weight, then tap through the photo checklist and Mark as Photographed
Tip: Take photos on iPhone → iCloud syncs → photos in Camera Roll on iPad when you list. Already shot them? Start from the Drive folder in 1.1 to auto-link.
▶

List & Publish

1
Tap List & Publish on the home screen
2
Pick the piece — status shown in brackets next to EB number
3
Set best display location, Indoor/Outdoor, care instructions, suggested display (colors & materials were already captured in New Piece)
4
Tap Generate Description — AI writes the title, description & SEO; edit inline or tap Fix
5
Upload photos (best first) — or they link automatically if you started from a Drive folder; pick a video here too
6
All green → tap Publish — goes live on Shopify instantly (any picked video uploads automatically)
Outdoor pot? Amber warning appears automatically — local delivery only, shipping handled for you.
✓

Mark as Sold

1
Tap Mark as Sold on home screen
2
Find piece by EB number or scroll
3
Choose channel: Shopify · Etsy · Craft Fair · Hair Salon · Direct · Custom
4
Tap Mark Sold — store and Airtable update at once
Note: Sold pieces stay on store in Portfolio with SOLD badge — your history builds automatically.

Craft Fair Mode

1
Before leaving home — tap Craft Fair Mode to load inventory offline
2
Search by EB number, type, or season
3
Tap a piece card to see full details
4
When it sells → tap card → Sold at craft fair
5
Syncs to store when back on Wi-Fi
Tip: Customers scan the QR tag on any piece — opens your store so they can see more.

If Something Looks Wrong

•
Stats show 0 / not loading? Tap the status pill top-right to reconnect — no token to enter, the server holds all credentials
•
Publish not working? Server may be asleep — wait 30 sec, retry
•
Description not generating? Same fix — wait 30 sec, retry
•
When in doubt — close Safari and reopen the Master Tool URL
Health check: eb-shopify-server.onrender.com/health — loads = working.
↻

Re-sync a Listed Piece

1
Changed a piece's details in Airtable/the tool after it was listed? Tap Re-sync on the home screen
2
Pick the published piece — re-applies its attributes to Shopify (adds only, never removes) and refreshes inventory
3
Leaves title, description, photos, and price untouched
Use it when: you added colors, materials, care, indoor/outdoor, suggested display, etc. to an already-live listing.

Log a Cost  — new

📸 Snap a receipt
1
Tap Log a cost → Snap or drop a receipt
2
Tap the upload box → Camera to snap, or Files for electronic receipts
3
Tap Parse with Claude AI — vendor, amount, date filled in automatically
4
Review the fields, pick a category, tap Save
Works with: JPG · PNG · PDF — printed or electronic receipts
🚗 Log mileage
1
Tap Log a cost → Log mileage
2
Type trip details OR upload a photo of handwritten mileage notes
3
Enter miles — dollar amount calculates automatically (IRS rate)
4
Pick purpose and tap Save mileage
IRS rate: $0.70/mile (2026) — calculated automatically
